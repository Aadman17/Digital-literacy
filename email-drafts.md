Dear Professor [Last Name],

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to respectfully request a short extension for the upcoming assignment submission deadline for [Course Name/Code], currently due on [original due date].

Over the past week, I have been dealing with [brief, genuine reason – e.g., a health issue / unexpected family circumstances / overlapping examination preparation], which has significantly impacted my ability to complete the assignment to the standard I would like to submit.

I sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and I am grateful for your understanding. Please let me know if you require any further information or if an alternative arrangement would be more suitable.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Yours sincerely,
